ABOUT US

Preserving Local History

In 2010, a passionate plant lover named Alisha envisioned a website that would serve as a one-stop destination for plant enthusiasts worldwide. Inspired by her own experiences and the growing interest in green living, Alisha set out to create a virtual platform that would provide comprehensive information, resources, and a sense of community for plant enthusiasts of all levels.
